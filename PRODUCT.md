Jameel First App is a mobile product designed to become an expert electric-vehicle hub for users who are currently exploring EV mobility in Italy, with a possible future evolution toward other languages and countries.

In the first discovery phase, Jameel Motors acts as the initial umbrella: it has its own Home Page to introduce the ecosystem and help the user choose between Zeekr and Geely. After the brand choice, the app foregrounds the vehicles, services, and network of the selected brand, because the user is mainly interested in understanding models, alternatives, and concrete possibilities.

When the user enters a more decision-oriented pre-purchase phase, Jameel Motors must become more visible as a reassurance element: assistance guarantee, maintenance, certified network, and continuity of support after purchase. The app must therefore make electric mobility simpler, more accessible, and more reliable without anticipating post-sales functionality that is not present in the MVP.

## 3. Vision

### Full Vision

Jameel First App must become the EV Expert Hub that accompanies the user through the electric journey across three phases: Pre-sales: Discovery & Evaluation; Post-sales: Order Tracking & Delivery; Post-sales: Vehicle Ownership.

The long-term promise is:

> Once inside the Jameel ecosystem, electric mobility becomes simpler and every touchpoint is trustworthy quality.

### MVP Vision

The August MVP focuses only on the Pre-sales: Discovery & Evaluation phase.

At launch, the app must offer an open, exploratory guest experience with intentionally limited functionality. The user enters from a Jameel Motors umbrella Home Page, chooses Zeekr or Geely, then discovers vehicles, services, network, and membership inside the Jameel space dedicated to the selected brand. Jameel Motors must remain clearly present as the ecosystem guarantor, while storytelling, images, and mood orient toward the vehicle brand identity.

## 4. Problem To Solve

The electric-vehicle discovery experience is currently fragmented.

Users find information across websites, social channels, dealer pages, events, and separate touchpoints. Distributors are often barely visible in the customer journey and rarely provide a recognizable digital ecosystem.

Jameel First App must solve this fragmentation by giving Jameel Motors an umbrella and guarantor role, without taking centrality away from the brands during discovery. After the choice between Geely and Zeekr, the focus must remain on the selected brand's products, services, and network. In the pre-purchase decision phase, Jameel Motors must emerge as a reliable connector between vehicles, services, dealers, workshops, assistance, and maintenance.

## 5. Objectives

### Primary Objectives

- Progressively position Jameel Motors as an authoritative EV expert in Italy: discreet in first discovery, more present in the pre-purchase decision phase.
- Simplify discovery of Geely and Zeekr electric vehicles.
- Make the connected EV-service offer clear.
- Highlight the certified dealer and workshop network.
- Generate qualified leads.

## 6. Audience

### Target Users

The MVP target splits into two main types: users transitioning from combustion to electric, and users who already drive electric vehicles and are changing vehicle.

#### Transitioning User, From Combustion To Electric

A user who has never owned an electric vehicle and is approaching the EV world for the first time.

Pain points:

- They do not understand the electric world, the technologies, or the differences from combustion vehicles.
- They do not know whether switching to electric is convenient for them.
- They do not know the available brands or which is best for their needs.
- They find fragmented, confusing, and unreliable information.

Main needs:

- Understand clearly and simply how the EV world works.
- Evaluate whether transitioning to electric is convenient for them.
- Compare brands and vehicles transparently.
- Feel guided and reassured in the choice.

Internal target nuances:

| Subcategory           | Characteristics                                                                                                                           |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| EV novice             | Does not yet know the technology, range, charging, or differences from combustion; needs simple and reassuring explanations.              |
| Informed EV evaluator | Has already gathered EV information but is looking for transparent comparison between brands, costs, benefits, and fit with their habits. |

#### Existing EV User, Vehicle Change

A user who already owns an electric vehicle and wants to change vehicle.

Pain points:

- Doubts about technology, range, and long-term vehicle reliability.
- Fear that spare parts do not exist or are hard to source for Chinese vehicles.
- Doubts about the quality and reliability of after-sales assistance.
- Fear of not having a structured support network across the territory.

Main needs:

- Certainty about the vehicle's quality, technology, and long-term performance.
- Original and certified spare parts, with guaranteed availability.
- High-quality, structured, and easily accessible after-sales assistance.
- A reliable and widespread dealer and workshop network.

Internal target nuances:

| Subcategory            | Characteristics                                                                                                                  |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Pragmatic EV owner     | Already has EV experience and wants certainty about real-world range, reliability, assistance, and ease of daily management.     |
| Tech-oriented EV owner | Already knows the EV world and mainly evaluates technology, performance, updates, build quality, and long-term support strength. |

## 7. Positioning

Jameel First App must not be only a car catalog, and it must not be only a dealer locator.

It must be positioned as:

- A reliable guide to electric mobility.
- A curated access point to Geely and Zeekr, with Jameel Motors as the progressive guarantor of the ecosystem.
- A practical tool for discovering vehicles, services, and network.
- A pre-sales bridge between interest and qualified contact.
- A foundation for a future complete EV ownership ecosystem.

## 8. Brand Feeling

The product must feel:

- Expert, but not cold.
- Clear, but not simplistic.
- Premium, but not distant.
- Useful, but not pushy.
- Reliable, but not bureaucratic.
- Modern and EV-native, but supported by concrete automotive distribution experience.
- New in the Italian EV landscape, but credible through competence, selection, and service quality.
- Reassuring in the decision phase, especially on assistance, maintenance, and continuity after purchase.

The user must feel guided, supported, and more confident. The app must reduce uncertainty around electric mobility and make Jameel Motors feel like a progressive guarantee: not necessarily the best-known brand during first discovery, but a solid partner when the user moves from curiosity toward a purchase decision.

The feeling must communicate that Jameel is not only aiming for quantity of territorial presence, but for quality of experience: competence, network reliability, assistance capability, and continuity over time must matter more than simple numerical coverage.

## 9. Voice And Register

### Default Register

The app is a product surface. It must prioritize clarity, orientation, task completion, and trust.

This means:

- Predictable navigation.
- Easy-to-scan content.
- Direct and readable actions.
- Clear and useful empty, loading, and error states.
- Editorial moments and motion only when they help comprehension.

### Voice

The voice must be:

- Expert.
- Reassuring.
- Concise.
- Practical.
- Transparent.
- Service-oriented.

### Copy Principles

- Use simple language for EV concepts.
- Explain benefits in terms of safety, daily usefulness, and trust.
- Avoid unverifiable promises.
- Avoid technical jargon unless it is explained.
- Avoid aggressive commercial language.
- Make the next step clear when the user is ready to act.

## 10. Product Principles

1. Guide before selling.
   The app must help the user understand, compare, and orient themselves before pushing contact actions.

2. Surface Jameel at the right moment.
   The product must keep Geely and Zeekr in the foreground during initial discovery and surface Jameel Motors in the decision phase, when reassurance, assistance, maintenance, and network quality matter.

3. Keep the MVP open.
   The August release must be explorable without login or account.

4. Reduce uncertainty around electric.
   Content, tools, and network must make EV adoption more concrete and manageable.

5. Connect discovery and action.
   Exploration of vehicles, services, and network must naturally lead to test drive, support, or lead capture.

6. Stay simple and shallow.
   The MVP architecture must avoid unnecessary complexity and keep the user close to the Jameel Motors Home Page, the selected brand, and the brand's main tabs.

7. Prepare the future without anticipating it.
   The MVP must not implement ownership, login, or order tracking, but must not block their future evolution.

## 13. Information Architecture

### Primary Navigation

The August MVP uses a two-level model:

1. **Jameel Motors Home Page**, a scrollable umbrella page with cross-brand content.
2. **Brand space**, the Jameel Motors space dedicated to the selected brand: Zeekr or Geely.

Inside the Jameel space dedicated to the brand, 4 main tabs are visible:

1. Home
2. Product
3. Network
4. Membership

The **Brand Switch** remains always visible across all tabs and lets the user change brand or return to the Jameel Motors Home Page.

### App Map - First Level

The MVP architecture is made of one Jameel Motors umbrella page and Jameel spaces dedicated to the vehicle brands.

```text
App download
|
+-- Jameel Motors Home Page
    |
    +-- Brand selection / Brand Switch
        |
        +-- Brand: Zeekr
        |   +-- Home
        |   +-- Product
        |   +-- Network
        |   +-- Membership
        |
        +-- Brand: Geely
            +-- Home
            +-- Product
            +-- Network
            +-- Membership
```

### Brand-Space Ownership Rule

When the user enters a vehicle brand space, they always remain inside the Jameel Motors app. Storytelling, images, and mood may orient toward the selected vehicle brand identity, but services, support, network, overall structure, and experience guarantee remain Jameel.

The brand space is not the vehicle brand's own app: it is the Jameel space dedicated to that brand.

### Main Content And Actions By Page

These elements are not sub-pages: they are content, internal sections, modules, or main actions present in the first-level pages.

| Page                    | Main content                                                                                                  | Main actions                                                                                                                                                    |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

Enter Product Services to see service details for the brand. |

### Progressive Experience Adaptation

The experience remains guest-only, but it can adapt content and priorities based on the selected brand and the user's interactions in the session or on the device.

| Key moment          | User signal                                                                  | Content adaptation                                                                                                         |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First access        | No previous interaction                                                      | Show Jameel Motors Home Page with cross-brand content and invitation to choose Zeekr or Geely.                             |
| Brand choice        | The user selects Zeekr or Geely                                              | Enter the Jameel space dedicated to the brand and show Home, Product, Network, and Membership with brand-specific content. |

| Model interest      | The user opens or interacts with Vehicle Detail                              | Give more prominence to test drive, lead capture, and the Route Planner connected to the vehicle, if planned.              |
| Network interaction | The user opens a Dealer/Workshop Detail from the map                         | Show location information and CTAs toward contact, test drive, lead, or related flows.                                     |
| Membership interest | The user opens Membership during or after an event                           | Show the defined mechanic, possible progress, unlockable benefits, or available in-app actions.                            |
| Conversion start    | The user starts test drive, lead capture, contact, or Membership interaction | Let the user complete the flow, show confirmation, and propose the coherent next step.                                     |

### Navigation Rules

- The app opens without authentication.
- The user can explore all MVP areas as a guest.
- The Jameel Motors Home Page is the cross-brand access point.
- After brand choice, the tab bar shows Home, Product, Network, and Membership.
- Brand Switch must remain always visible across all tabs.
- Conversion actions can appear contextually in Brand Home, Product, Network, and Membership.
- Membership is a space to be defined around summer events, with possible gamification or unlockable in-app benefits.
- Future logged-in functionality must remain separate from the guest MVP experience.

## 15. Main Flows

### Flow 1 - First Exploration

1. The user downloads and opens the app.
2. They enter the guest experience.
3. They see the Jameel Motors Home Page as the cross-brand umbrella page.
4. They choose a brand, Zeekr or Geely.
5. They enter the Jameel space dedicated to the brand and browse Home, Product, Network, or Membership.
6. When ready, they move to test drive, lead, contact, or Membership interaction if the event mechanic is available.

### Flow 2 - Vehicle Discovery Toward Test Drive

1. The user selects Zeekr or Geely.
2. They open Product.
3. They choose the Vehicles path.
4. They explore the selected brand's vehicles using official brand-site content.
5. They open Vehicle Detail.
6. If useful, they use the Route Planner connected to the vehicle detail.
7. They choose to request a test drive or leave a lead.
8. Alternatively, from Product they can enter Product Services to see service details for the brand.
9. They enter the required data when starting a lead or test-drive flow.
10. They receive a clear confirmation.

### Flow 3 - Network Discovery

1. The user selects Zeekr or Geely.
2. They open Network.
3. They see a map with dealers and workshops for the selected brand.
4. They select a dealer or workshop.
5. They open Dealer/Workshop Detail.
6. They start contact or a related CTA, if planned.

### Flow 4 - Membership As Event Download Hook

1. The user encounters Jameel during a summer event in Italy or through a touchpoint connected to the event.
2. They download the app to access a mechanic or a benefit potentially unlockable in the app.
3. They enter the Jameel Motors Home Page and choose a brand.
4. They open Membership.
5. They see the mechanic defined for the MVP, if available.
6. They can unlock or request a possible benefit and are guided toward product discovery, test drive, or lead capture.

## 18. States And Edge Cases

### Global States

- First access: the user enters as a guest and immediately understands the ecosystem value.
- Empty state: if dealers, workshops, vehicles, or campaigns are unavailable, explain the situation and offer a useful action.
- Loading: short, clear, reassuring states.
- Error: explain what happened and offer retry or contact.
- Permission denied: if location is denied, allow manual search.
- Offline: preserve basic orientation where possible and clarify which actions require connection.
- Success: confirm form submission and state what happens next.

### Product Edge Cases

- The user does not grant location access.
- The user starts a lead form but leaves required fields empty.
- The user wants a test drive but has not selected a dealer.
- The user has not selected a brand yet.
- The selected brand does not have complete or updated content from the official source.
- The Membership/event mechanic is not yet defined or active.
- Brand Switch does not correctly preserve the current brand.
- The user expects ownership features that are not present in the MVP.

## 19. Conversion Strategy

The main objective for the MVP in this phase is to obtain a high number of app downloads. The first conversion to optimize is therefore bringing users who encounter Jameel Motors, during events or other touchpoints to be identified, to download the app and start the guest experience.

Once the app is downloaded, the subsequent conversion goals remain valid: brand choice, qualified first contact, test-drive request, dealer/workshop contact, and interaction with possible Membership/event mechanics, without making the experience feel like a simple lead form.

Primary MVP conversion:

- Download the app after contact with Jameel Motors through an event, campaign, physical touchpoint, or digital channel to be defined.
- Choose a brand, Zeekr or Geely, from the Jameel Motors Home Page.

Main conversion actions:

- Request a test drive.
- Request first commercial contact.
- Contact a dealer.
- Open Vehicle Detail and Route Planner.
- Interact with Membership once the event mechanic is defined.

Conversion must emerge as a natural step after:

- Vehicle exploration.
- Service exploration.
- Dealer/workshop discovery.
- Event or campaign participation.
- Interest in Membership or event benefits.
